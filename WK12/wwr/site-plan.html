Vanuatu is a land of volcanoes and magic, underwater shipwrecks, ancient art and dance, waterfalls and blue holes, organic foods and a myriad of hidden bays and beaches. Discover the lush tropical islands, warm turquoise waters, the friendly people, the colours, the culture and the fantastic food. Originally inhabited by people from Melanesia, Vanuatu had its first European settlers in the 19th century. France and the UK claimed some of the land and it was called the New Hebrides until Vanuatu’s gain its independence in the 1980. Made I made up of 83 small islands, the largest island is Espiritu Santo and the capital city is Port Vila and is located on the island of Efate. here is so much to see and do on the beautiful islands of Vanuatu. Although famous for its beaches, there is more to this tropical paradise than swimming and surfing. To really experience the magic of the colourful underwater world, diving gets you up close and personal with some of the world’s best dive sites. Pick up some souvenirs at the mamas local markets or spoil yourself at boutique shops, attend a vibrant festival or event or join a scenic cruise around the islands. However you wish to spend your time, you can sight see at your own pace or join one of the many Vanuatu adventours and activities to get all the inside knowledge from the locals. blue Lagoon is Cool Crystal Blue Water Pool lying amidst the natural green forest. It is a forty-five minutes’ drive from Port Vila city to Blue Lagoon. Blue Lagoon is located on the eastern side of Efate. The coolness of this unique blue water pool must only be experienced to remember in life. This is for people who want to have an unforgettable experience swimming and cooling down from the tropical temperature in a crystal Blue Water pool with natural green forest surroundings.
